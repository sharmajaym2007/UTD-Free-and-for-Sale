UTD Free and for Sales
This version of UTD Craiglist lets you surf different products which students and UTD
surplus has to offer. You can surf, search products on the basis of category, upload
pics of your product to sell and contact the seller if you like to buy.

User discretion is advised. Don't annoy the sellers by useless Emails.
